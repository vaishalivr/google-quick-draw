And as I write this, the dishwasher that comes to your mind may not be the same as the dishwasher is someone else’s mind.

Or a leaf in Canada may not be the same as the leaf that most people think of.

You can hover over the doodles above to see what they actually are.

The image that comes to mind when these words are mentioned, has a lot to do with the version of these words we’ve grown up with.

Here I attempt to explore the Google's Quick Draw dataset which is the worlds largest published doodle dataset containing 50 million drawings belonging to different categories such as an axe, a football, a flower, a zigzag and even the Eiffel Tower. Quick Draw is a game about a neural network learning how to recognize doodles where these doodles are used to advance machine learning research.
